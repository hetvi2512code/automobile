OUR JOURNEY

The special fascination of the BMW Group not only lies in its products and technology, but also in the company’s history, written by inventors, pioneers and brilliant designers. Today, the BMW Group, with more than 30 production and assembly facilities as well as a global sales network, is the world’s leading manufacturer of premium automobiles and motorcycles, and provider of premium financial and mobility services. . At the same time, flexibility and continuous optimisation of value chains ensure competitiveness.

INNOVATION AND DESIGN

Always staying one step ahead and shaping the mobility of the future is an important part of our company’s philosophy. We therefore work tirelessly to find innovative solutions today for the issues of tomorrow. Our evolution began in 1972, with the first all-electric BMW. From those innovative beginnings to the cutting-edge technology of BMW i, electrified efficiency and performance have defined the future of BMW. The BMW Group sets trends in production technology and sustainability as an innovation leader with an intelligent material mix, a technological shift towards digitalisation and resource-efficient production.
